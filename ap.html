Welcome to Flyworld International

Aviation, Hospitality, Travel & Tourism, and Logistics industry has an excellent growth rate in India and internationally. These industries are always interested to hire skilled, talented, trained people in each and every role from cabin crew to ground staff from airport management to airline ticketing & cargo handling and at the same time, the hotels & hospitality, travel & tourism, shipping, logistics also having similar growth.

According to Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA), the Civil Aviation Industry in India has emerged as one of the fastest growing Aviation Industries since 2013. India is currently considered the 5th largest domestic Civil Aviation Market in the World & will soon become 3rd largest.

The International Air Transport Association (IATA) has predicted a rapid growth in India in all the coming years No other industry offers career benefits and high salary, as lucrative as these industries.

Flyworld Institute,can deliver an inspiring training course designed to suit students from different educational backgrounds. We have started Flyworld Institute to train candidates seeing the global demand for Cabin Crew/Flight Steward, Ground staff, Hospitality, Travel & Tourism and logistic industrries and we assist them in placements in different national and international Organizations.

Our trainned, experienced faculty focuses on honing your skills and abilities. Flyworld institute is one of the front runners in the arena of job oriented education in Madhya Pradesh.
